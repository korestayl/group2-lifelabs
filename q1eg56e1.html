HOW WILL I FIND A NEED OF VIEWERS IN A STREAMING CATEGORY?

WHAT, HOW, WHY

WHAT distracts the students?

There are many things that distract students, the main variety of them are video games and one of them is specifically FPS gaming. Ever since the pandemic started, FPS games have started to get even more notice/usages from students due to lockdowns or home quarantine.

HOW does this affect the students?

The students get distracted. It makes students play too much or be addicted to it. It has affected their daily lives so much to the point that they can be irresponsible, poor at academic performance (procrastinate), and will easily decline in any interest in academic activities.

WHY should this be concerning?

It should be concerning because they are constantly distracted/addicted by what they play, with it they negatively develop a habit that affects their academics, and maybe even family or friend matters.

5 WHYS

TOPIC: FPS GAMES ARE ADDICTING

WHY are FPS games addicting?

- Because they feature this satisfaction of achievement and superiority. It also gives dynamism and unpredictable elements that drive sports competition.

WHY do they show these elements to games?

- It is to attract audiences to like and play their games.

WHY do they want audiences to like and play their games?

- Because game developers/companies want their games to be at the top in popularity and usages.

WHY do they want their games to gain in popularity and usages?

- It is so for their company/developers to gain income.

WHY do they need to gain income?

- To support their daily consumption of goods and services as well as to keep the business afloat.
