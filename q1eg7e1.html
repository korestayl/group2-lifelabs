HOW DO I DOCUMENT THE JOURNEY IN FINDING THE NEED IN STREAMING?

WHAT IS STREAMING?

- Streaming is the delivery of live or recorded media material to computers and mobile devices through the internet and playback in real time.

TYPES OF STREAMING CONTENTS

- Podcasts, webcasts, movies, TV shows and music videos are common forms of streaming content.

PLATFORMS USED FOR STREAMING

- Examples of platforms used for streaming are: Youtube, Facebook Watch/Live, Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, iQIYI, Hulu, Crunchyroll, Bilibili, HBO Max, Amazon Music, Apple Music, Spotify, Youtube Music and more.

NEEDS OF VIEWERS

- The genuine engagement enabled by streaming fosters a relationship with viewers. Being relatable is critical for every content creator. Though it may be more difficult for companies because they must demonstrate their human aspect without appearing "human." A brand's personality must be in sync with its audience.

CHARACTERISTICS OF A GOOD STREAM

- It Should Be Engaging

The key to good content is viewer attentiveness. With shortening attention spans, it is critical to display information in a way that keeps the viewer engaged for as long as possible.

- It Should Be Aesthetically And Sonically Pleasing

The attraction of a video is another component that adds to its success. The way it appears and sounds has a lot to do with how it is accepted. Boring graphics may turn off viewers, causing them to become distracted and miss essential information. If the images are very complex and confusing, it may also be distracting. So striking a balance is critical. The audio is the same.
