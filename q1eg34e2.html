HOW DO I DETERMINE WHAT CONTENT IS SUITABLE
FOR DIFFERENT TYPES OF AUDIENCE?

- Analyze all of your content, determine what the audience liked most, and focus on that. Infographics, reels, advice, listings, and even exams are examples of this. Second, you may simply repurpose or reprint previously successful material. It worked before, therefore it should work again this time. Personas for your target audiences are almost certainly already in place. Once you've identified your target demographic, you may create marketing personas. Personas, in general, make the audience more approachable while providing a deeper grasp of their interests, wants, concerns, sufferings, and preferences.
